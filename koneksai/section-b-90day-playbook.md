# KoneksAI — 90-Day Weekly Playbook
**13-Week Execution Guide | Port-au-Prince, Haiti**

---

## How to Use This Playbook

- **Automated tasks** = triggered by n8n, Google Apps Script, or scheduled WhatsApp/email sequences. Once built, they run without founder time.
- **Manual tasks** = require founder attention and judgment.
- **Time estimates** are per day, not per task. Total daily time target: 4–6 hours in Weeks 1–4; 5–7 hours in Weeks 5–12.
- **Success criteria** are measured at end of each week on Friday.
- Currency: USD unless stated. MonCash = Haitian mobile money.
