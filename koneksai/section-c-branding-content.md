### Tone Guide

**Core personality:** KoneksAI speaks like the smart cousin who studied tech abroad but came home and genuinely wants to help the family business grow. Not condescending. Not overly corporate. Real, warm, direct.

**Warm but Professional**
Write the way a trusted advisor talks — confident without being cold. Use first names when possible. Avoid jargon. If you must use a tech term, explain it in the next sentence.

- DO: "WhatsApp will answer your customers automatically — even at 2am while you sleep."
- DON'T: "Our AI-driven conversational commerce platform optimizes customer engagement touchpoints."

**Local and Grounded**
Reference real Haitian context: MonCash, Digicel, local market hours, power cuts, school schedules, market days. Show you understand how Port-au-Prince actually works.

- DO: "Even when BLECK (blackout) hits, your WhatsApp bot keeps responding."
- DON'T: "Ensure continuous uptime across all customer interaction channels."

**Trustworthy**
Haiti has been burned by scams and empty promises. Lead with proof. Show results before asking for money. Offer a demo. Let the work speak.

- DO: "Here's a screenshot of 47 orders a Pétion-Ville restaurant received through their WhatsApp bot last week."
- DON'T: "Join thousands of businesses revolutionizing their customer experience."

**Results-First**
Every piece of communication should answer: "What does this mean for MY money, MY time, MY customers?" Lead with the outcome, then explain the method.

- DO: "Imagine never missing a customer inquiry again — even on Sunday mornings."
- DON'T: "Our platform leverages machine learning to enhance response automation."

### Logo Description

**Concept:** The KoneksAI wordmark pairs bold modern typography with a symbol that fuses communication and technology — a WhatsApp-style speech bubble containing a geometric circuit node at its center. This signals: "This is WhatsApp. This is AI. This is yours."

**Typography:** Poppins Bold, tracked +20 (slightly spread for readability at small sizes). "Koneks" in `#1A6B3A` (Haitian green), "AI" in `#FFD700` (gold). No space between the two words — they are one identity.

**Symbol:** A rounded-corner speech bubble (WhatsApp proportion, 1:1 ratio, rounded corners at 30% of width) in `#1A6B3A`, containing a 4-point circuit node (cross shape with small circles at each endpoint) in `#FFD700`. The symbol sits to the LEFT of the wordmark.

**Lockup options:**
1. Horizontal: [symbol] + [wordmark] — primary usage
2. Stacked: [symbol above] + [wordmark below] — for square formats
3. Wordmark only: [KoneksAI in full type] — for small sizes, watermarks
4. Icon only: [symbol alone] — for app icons, favicons, WhatsApp profile photo

**Clear space:** Minimum clear space equal to the height of the "K" on all sides.

**Do not:**
- Stretch or rotate the logo
- Change individual letter colors
- Place on busy photographic backgrounds without a container
- Use the gold "AI" on yellow or white backgrounds (insufficient contrast)
