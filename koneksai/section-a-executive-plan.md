## Target Market

**Primary segment:** Port-au-Prince SMEs with 1–15 employees who use WhatsApp as their primary customer channel but have zero automation.

| Vertical | Specific Type | Why They Buy |
|---|---|---|
| Restaurants & food delivery | Informal restaurants, traiteurs, fritay stands with delivery | Orders come via WhatsApp; missed messages = lost revenue same day |
| Pharmacies | Independent pharmacies, not chains | Prescription inquiries, product availability, hours — volume is high, staff is stretched |
| Boutiques & retail | Clothing stores, cosmetics, accessory shops | Product catalog questions, price checks, "avèk sa ou genyen?" all day |
| Salons & beauty | Hair salons, barbershops, nail studios | Appointment booking is entirely WhatsApp-based; double-bookings and no-shows kill revenue |
| Schools & tutoring | Private primary/secondary schools, lesson centers | Parent inquiries about fees, schedules, registration — repetitive and time-consuming |
| Clinics & medical | Private clinics, dentists, general practitioners | Appointment requests, lab result follow-ups, hours — HIPAA-lite sensitivity a plus |

**Qualifying characteristics:**
- Already has a WhatsApp number (personal or Business) used for customer contact
- Receives 10+ customer WhatsApp messages per day and misses some
- Owner or manager responds manually from a personal phone
- Monthly revenue between $500–$15,000 USD (can pay $50–$100/mo without hardship)
- Located in Port-au-Prince metro: Pétion-Ville, Delmas, Tabarre, Turgeau, Carrefour, Croix-des-Bouquets

**Market size estimate:** ~18,000 registered SMEs in Port-au-Prince metro; ~30% use WhatsApp as a customer channel actively = ~5,400 addressable businesses. Target: 0.37% penetration at Day 90 (20 clients).

## Value Proposition

> **KoneksAI sets up a complete, automated WhatsApp Business system in 5 days — so a Port-au-Prince SME captures every customer inquiry, 24/7, without hiring extra staff.**

Specific before/after outcomes:

| Without KoneksAI | With KoneksAI |
|---|---|
| Customers wait 2–8 hours for a reply | Auto-reply in under 5 seconds, 24/7 |
| No product catalog; owner types prices manually each time | Structured WhatsApp catalog with photos, prices, categories |
| Zero appointment system; double-bookings common | Google Sheets booking system, confirmation sent automatically |
| Payment discussions are informal; MonCash links sent manually | MonCash payment link auto-generated and sent with order summary |
| Owner answers the same 10 questions 40x/week | FAQ auto-responder handles 80% of routine inquiries |
| Business closes at 6 PM; no after-hours capture | After-hours auto-reply captures lead info for next-day follow-up |

**Quantified claim:** Based on comparable automations in Caribbean/LATAM markets, clients typically see 40–60% reduction in manual reply time and recover 2–4 missed sales per week (valued at $20–$200 each depending on vertical).

## Top Risks

| # | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Security/political instability disrupts client operations** | High (40%) | High — clients close or pause; churn spikes | Build 3-month retainer prepayment option; diversify across 3+ verticals; keep $200 emergency reserve |
| 2 | **Meta restricts WhatsApp Business API in Haiti or raises costs** | Medium (25%) | High — core product breaks | Build on WhatsApp Business App first; n8n + Google Apps Script works without API; keep API as upsell |
| 3 | **Founder hits capacity ceiling before 20 clients** | Medium (30%) | Medium — revenue growth stalls | Templatize every setup into a playbook by Week 4; recruit 1 part-time assistant at $150/month if >12 clients |
| 4 | **Clients don't renew after setup; low retainer adoption** | Medium (20%) | Medium — kills MRR model | Mandate a 1-month retainer trial included in setup fee; show ROI via monthly report |
| 5 | **Competitors (freelancers, agencies) undercut on price** | Low-Medium (20%) | Low — market is underdeveloped | Compete on speed (5-day delivery), local MonCash/cash payment, Haitian Creole support, personal service |
